The Road to Reclaiming the Throne

Players will assume the role of the awakened King of Salted Fish, embarking on an epic journey to reclaim the throne. With bullet hell shooting as the core gameplay mechanics, warriors must navigate through ever-evolving challenges. As the game progresses, enemies grow stronger in both numbers and abilities, demanding masterful dodging skills and strategic counterattacks. Will you rise to become the true sovereign of the battlefield?

üëæ Boss fight
